Frequently asked questions

Install

How do I install the app?

Installation takes a few clicks from the Shopify App Store. Once installed, the app integrates with your store automatically.

Is the app compatible with my theme?

Yes — Style Lab Virtual Try-On is compatible with every Shopify 2.0 theme. The widget plugs in automatically through our theme app extension.

How it works

How does the virtual try-on work?

Shoppers upload a photo or use their camera. Our AI analyzes the silhouette and overlays the garment realistically.

How long does a generation take?

Generations typically take 30 seconds to 2 minutes, depending on image complexity and server load.

Billing

When will I be billed?

Pricing is pay-per-success: 1 generation = 1 successful try-on on your storefront. Failed AI generations never consume credits. See the pricing section for plan details.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — you can cancel your subscription at any time from your Shopify dashboard. No long-term commitment.
