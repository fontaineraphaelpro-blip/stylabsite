Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

Style Lab Virtual Try-On ("Stylab", "we", "our", "us") provides an AI virtual try-on app for Shopify stores. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, retain, and share personal data when merchants install the app and when their customers use the try-on widget.

Data controller: Style Lab
Contact: privacy@stylelab.com

1. Who this policy applies to

  • Merchants who install Stylab on a Shopify store (shop owner and staff account data).
  • End customers ("shoppers") who upload a photo to generate a try-on preview on a merchant's product page.

Merchants are responsible for their own storefront privacy notices and for informing shoppers that try-on involves photo processing.

2. Data we collect

Merchant / shop data

  • Shop domain, app installation status, and Shopify session data required to operate the app
  • Billing and subscription information processed through Shopify
  • Product IDs, handles, and optional flat-lay garment images you upload for try-on
  • Widget settings (colors, copy, enabled products)

Shopper try-on data

  • Photos uploaded for try-on — processed to generate a preview; not stored in Stylab's database after processing (see section 4)
  • Product context — product ID, handle, and public product image URL used for generation
  • Technical metadata — IP address (for rate limiting and abuse prevention), generation success/failure, latency, timestamps
  • Generated preview URL — a link to the AI output may be stored in server logs for analytics and support

Usage and analytics

  • Try-on session counts, add-to-cart events after try-on, A/B test buckets, and credit usage

3. How we use data

  • Provide, operate, and improve the virtual try-on service
  • Bill merchants through Shopify based on successful try-on generations
  • Enforce quotas, rate limits, and fraud prevention
  • Provide merchant analytics in the Shopify admin
  • Respond to support requests and legal obligations
  • Handle GDPR-related requests from Shopify (data export and deletion webhooks)

We do not sell personal data.

4. Shopper photos — processing and storage

When a shopper uploads a photo:

  1. The photo is sent over HTTPS from the shopper's browser to our application backend.
  2. Our backend forwards the image to Replicate (our AI inference provider) to generate the try-on preview.
  3. The preview URL is returned to the shopper's browser and displayed in the widget.

What we do not store: original shopper photos are not saved in Stylab's PostgreSQL database after processing completes.

What Replicate temporarily holds: during inference, Replicate receives the shopper photo and product image. Per Replicate's documentation for API predictions:

  • Files uploaded via Replicate's file API typically expire after 24 hours.
  • Output files served at replicate.delivery typically expire after about 1 hour for API-created predictions.

The widget displays a "photo not stored" notice to shoppers. Merchants should link to this policy from their storefront privacy notice where required.

5. Data retention

Data typeRetention
Original shopper photosNot retained in Stylab's database after processing. Temporary copies at Replicate per their retention policy (see section 4).
Generated preview URLs in try-on logsStored while the app is installed, for analytics and support. The underlying Replicate-hosted file usually expires within ~1 hour.
Try-on usage logs (IP, product, success, latency)Retained while the app is installed on the merchant's shop.
Merchant shop settings and product configurationRetained while the app is installed.
All merchant data after app uninstallDeleted when the app is uninstalled, via our uninstall and GDPR webhooks. Residual backups purged within 30 days.

7. GDPR and your rights

Stylab supports Shopify's mandatory GDPR webhooks:

  • customers/data_request — export of customer-related try-on log data tied to a Shopify customer ID
  • customers/redact — deletion of try-on logs associated with a customer
  • shop/redact — deletion of all shop data after uninstall

Under the GDPR, you may have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or object to processing, and to data portability where applicable.

Merchants can contact us at privacy@stylelab.com. Shoppers should contact the merchant whose store they used; merchants may forward requests to us.

8. Security

We use HTTPS for data in transit, access controls on our infrastructure, and industry-standard practices to protect data against unauthorized access, loss, or alteration.

9. Cookies

The try-on widget and Shopify admin may use essential cookies or local storage required for the service to function. We do not use advertising or cross-site tracking cookies in the Stylab widget.

10. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Material changes will be published on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
