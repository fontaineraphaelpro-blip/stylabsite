Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 15, 2026

Style Lab Virtual Try-On ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal data.

1. Data collected

We collect the following data:

  • Shopify account data: store name, email, billing information
  • Shopper photos: used only to render the virtual try-on; deleted immediately after processing
  • Usage data: widget usage statistics, number of try-ons
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, pages visited

2. Use of data

We use your data to:

  • Provide and improve our virtual try-on service
  • Process payments and manage your subscription
  • Send important service-related notifications
  • Analyze service usage to improve features
  • Comply with our legal obligations

3. Data sharing

We never sell your personal data. We may share data with:

  • Service providers: hosting (AWS), image processing (Replicate, Imgur)
  • Shopify: for store integration
  • Legal authorities: when required by law

4. Data retention

Shopper photos are deleted immediately after processing. Other data is retained for as long as necessary to provide the service or to comply with legal obligations.

5. Your rights (GDPR)

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Rectify your data
  • Erase your data
  • Object to processing
  • Data portability
  • Withdraw your consent

To exercise these rights, contact us at: privacy@stylelab.com

6. Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or destruction.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for the service to function. We do not use advertising or tracking cookies.

8. Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page with a new last-updated date.

9. Contact

For any question about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
