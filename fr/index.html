How it works

How Stylab works

From install to measurable shopper engagement — four straightforward steps for Shopify apparel brands.

1. Add the try-on button

Install the Shopify app and enable Stylab on selected products.

2. Shopper uploads a photo

Customers click “Try it on” and upload a photo from mobile or desktop.

3. AI generates the preview

Stylab creates a virtual try-on image using your product photo.

4. Track the impact

See try-ons, add-to-cart actions, and usage from your dashboard.

Confidentialité

Confidentialité matters when shoppers upload photos

Stylab is designed to process shopper photos only for generating the try-on preview. Make privacy clear to your customers and keep your store experience trustworthy.

How shopper photos are handled

  • Shopper photos are processed for try-on generation
  • Original shopper photos are not stored after processing
  • The widget displays a clear “photo not stored” message to shoppers

What may be stored for analytics

  • Generated try-on image URLs
  • Usage events (success, latency, product viewed)
  • Your flat-lay garment photos in Shopify Files

FAQ

Questions fréquentes des marchands Shopify

Do I need to edit my Shopify theme? +
No. Stylab Virtual Try-On is designed to install through Shopify and add a try-on experience to product pages without manual theme editing.
Which products work best? +
Stylab is best suited for apparel products such as t-shirts, hoodies, jerseys, dresses, and other visual fashion items where shoppers want to see style and fit before buying.
Do failed generations use credits? +
Only successful try-ons should count toward your quota. Failed generations can be retried without consuming a successful try-on credit.
Are shopper photos stored? +
Stylab is designed to process photos for generation only. The widget tells shoppers their photo is not stored. See our privacy policy for full details.
Can I choose which products use try-on? +
Yes. You can enable or disable try-on by product from your Shopify admin — useful for catalogs with mixed product types.
Can I measure performance? +
Yes. Stylab includes analytics for try-on sessions, usage, add-to-cart activity, and A/B test buckets so you can see what happens after shoppers try on.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. The try-on experience is designed for mobile and desktop shoppers on standard Shopify product pages.
Can I try it before installing? +
Yes. Use the live demo on this page or visit our demo store REMADE ICONS to see the same widget in a real Shopify storefront.
How much does it cost? +
Plans start free (50 successful try-ons/month). Paid plans: Starter $19, Growth $49, Scale $149. All billing runs through Shopify. See pricing for details.
