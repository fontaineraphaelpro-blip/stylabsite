Privacy

Privacy matters when shoppers upload photos

Photos are used only to generate the try-on preview. We do not store original shopper photos in our database.

What happens to shopper photos

  • Processed securely to generate the try-on preview
  • Original photos are not saved in Stylab’s database after processing
  • Transit through our AI provider (Replicate) for inference only
  • Widget shows a clear “photo not stored” notice before upload

What we keep for your store

  • Usage events (sessions, success rate, product viewed)
  • Generated preview URLs in server logs (Replicate links expire ~1 h)
  • Your flat-lay garment photos in Shopify Files
