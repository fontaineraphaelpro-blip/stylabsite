When Stylab is a better fit

Choose Stylab if you want native A/B testing, pay only for successful generations, and per-product flat-lay control — especially for streetwear and jerseys — without promising guaranteed conversion lifts.

When Genlook may fit

Choose Genlook if email capture during try-on, Klaviyo sync, Genlook Studio, or their revenue simulator are higher priorities. Test both on the same product photos.
